Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

PlayStadium.io is a points-based recreational app. There is no real money involved: points cannot be purchased with real money and cannot be cashed out. This policy explains what data we collect when you use the app and how we handle it.

What we collect

  • Email address — when you sign in with Google, Google shares your email with us so we can identify your account.
  • Gameplay activity — bets placed, outcomes, balance history, and session data. This is the core record of your account.
  • IP address and basic request metadata — logged automatically by our hosting provider (Vercel) for security and abuse prevention.

We do not collect payment information, SSN, date of birth, or any financial data. There is no real-money path.

How we use it

  • Running your account: tracking your points balance, bet history, and preferences.
  • Security: detecting abuse, rate-limiting, and preventing fraudulent activity.
  • Product improvement: understanding how the app is used in aggregate (no individual profiling or advertising).

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for behavioral profiling.

Who we share it with

We use the following sub-processors to operate the service:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and real-time infrastructure. Data stored in US East (AWS).
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — hosting and serverless functions. Logs are retained per Vercel's standard policy.
  • Google (google.com) — OAuth identity provider. Your Google account email is shared with us at sign-in; we do not receive your Google password or any other Google account data.

Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data (email, gameplay history) within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or security reasons.

Your rights

You can request:

  • Export — a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — removal of your account and all associated data.
  • Correction — updating any incorrect information.

To exercise these rights, email us at josephpateickwolf2008@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage to remember your session and preferences (sound settings, active player). We use session cookies set by Supabase Auth for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Children

PlayStadium.io is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

AI disclosure

This app was developed with assistance from AI tools (Anthropic Claude). Your gameplay data is not shared with any AI system and is not used to train AI models.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.
